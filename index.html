About stadiastats.jdeslip.com
This website tracks various Stadia and Stadia community related metrics over time including follower growth for various Stadia-centric content creators and personalities who spread positivity about the platform.

A small amount of historical data is from SocialBlade.com - For example, https://socialblade.com/youtube/c/stadiacast/monthly
